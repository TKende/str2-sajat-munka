WINGBACK CHAIR

This accent chair—which can range from classic to contemporary—is marked by its statement side panels, which extend from the (often tall) chair back.

CLUB CHAIR

Spacious, plush, and typically sold in leather, the club chair is a classic piece of living room furniture that is designed with rounded curves to optimize comfort.

WINDSOR CHAIR

Sunken saddle seats, solid wood design, and a round-tenoned back and legs are just a few of the Windsor chair's defining characteristics. The chair has inspired many modern iterations, and is often used in dining rooms.
